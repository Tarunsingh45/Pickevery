Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

1. Scope

This policy applies to delivery and ride-sharing requests placed via Pickevery.

2. Delivery Requests

  • Once a traveler accepts a delivery request, cancellations by the requester are not permitted except by mutual agreement.
  • If a delivery is not completed due to traveler fault or service failure, users may request a refund. Refunds will be processed after verification.
  • Refunds, if approved, are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days (processed via PayU).

3. Ride-Sharing

  • Requesters may cancel a confirmed ride if the cancellation adheres to any in-app timings (if specified).
  • Travelers should avoid last-minute cancellations. Cancellations without valid reasons may incur penalties.
  • Refunds for canceled rides will be processed within 5–7 business days after verification.

4. Platform Fees

Platform or service fees charged by Pickevery are generally non-refundable once a request is confirmed.

5. Disputes

For disputes, please contact pickevery.official@gmail.com. We will investigate and take appropriate action, which may include holding payouts until resolution.
