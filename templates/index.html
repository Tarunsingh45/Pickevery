Why rural communities are disconnected

Essential goods and same-day travel are often unavailable for people living outside cities. Pickevery targets that gap by coordinating travelers and local demand.

Delayed essentials

Medicines and urgent items can take hours or days to reach remote locations.

High travel costs

Traveling to the city for small items is expensive and time-consuming.

No delivery system

Traditional couriers rarely serve very remote villages.

Underused routes

Many travelers pass through rural areas but there's no easy way to coordinate help.

How Pickevery Works

A simple 3-step flow matching local needs with passing travelers.

1

Villager requests

Submit requests for urgent items or seats via the app.

2

Traveler matches

Verified travelers on the same route see matching requests and accept them.

3

Mission complete

Delivery or shared ride completed; traveler earns a small payout.
