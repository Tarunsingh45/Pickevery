Why Rural Communities Are Disconnected

Delayed Essentials

Medicines, books, and groceries arrive late or not at all in rural areas.

High Travel Costs

Villagers spend significant money traveling to towns for basic needs.

No Delivery System

Traditional delivery services don't reach remote rural areas.

Missed Opportunities

Many travelers pass through villages daily but can't help due to lack of coordination.

How PickEvery Works

A simple 3-step process that connects rural needs with urban travelers

1

Villager Requests

Rural community members submit requests for essential goods or rides through our simple app interface.

2

Traveler Matches

Verified travelers heading to rural areas see matching requests along their route and choose to help.

3

Mission Complete

Traveler delivers goods or provides ride, earns incentives, and creates positive community impact.

About Us

Founded on Purpose

Pickevery was founded in early 2025 by two passionate co-founders who recognized the deep gap in rural access and the untapped potential of everyday travel.

We believe that technology should bridge divides, not create them. Our platform turns ordinary journeys into extraordinary opportunities for community support and rural empowerment.

Why PickEvery is Different
  • No need for delivery fleets or warehouses
  • Built for underserved areas (rural-first approach)
  • Micro-earning opportunities for travelers
  • Community and impact-focused mission
Our Mission

To create a sustainable, community-driven ecosystem where every journey has the potential to make a positive impact on rural communities across India.
